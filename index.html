Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Dreamore: Ghibli Generator application ("App").

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following types of information:

  • User-Provided Images: The App processes images you provide for transformation into Ghibli-style artwork.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage information to improve the App, such as transformation counts and feature usage.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device (such as device type, operating system version) may be collected for troubleshooting and optimization.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App.
  • Process and transform images as requested by you.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to enhance user experience.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent technical issues.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the App, such as cloud storage providers to store images temporarily during processing.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Images

Images you upload for transformation are processed on our servers and temporarily stored during processing. Original and transformed images are not permanently stored on our servers after the transformation process is complete, unless explicitly saved by you through the App's functionality.

In-App Purchases

Our App offers in-app purchases. Payment information is processed by Apple and subject to their privacy policy. We receive only purchase verification information, not your payment details.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on this page.
